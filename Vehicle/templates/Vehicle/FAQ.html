Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the Vehicle Parking System work?

The Vehicle Parking System efficiently manages the parking of bikes and cars by providing a digital platform for users to reserve parking spaces, make payments, and receive confirmation details.

2. Can I reserve a parking space in advance?

Yes, the system allows users to reserve parking spaces in advance through the online platform. Users can choose the date and time for their parking reservation.

3. How do I make payments for parking?

The payment process is user-friendly. You can make payments online using various payment methods such as credit/debit cards or digital wallets when reserving a parking space.

4. Is there a membership program for frequent users?

Yes, we offer a membership program for frequent users. Members enjoy exclusive benefits such as discounts, priority parking, and special promotions.
